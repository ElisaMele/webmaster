Get Involved

Saving Lives. Together.

Our mission of People Rescuing Animals … Animals Rescuing People wouldn’t be possible without thousands of dedicated volunteers and supporters. Whether you’re looking to volunteer, attend a gala, train your dog, or go bargain hunting, there are so many ways to get involved while supporting our lifesaving programs.

Attend an Event

Your gift makes a life-changing difference for animals and people in need.

Enroll in Training

Train for a lifetime of happiness with your pet at one of our classes or workshops.

Shop for a Cause

Save lives through your everyday purchases by shopping at our thrift store, pet boutique, and more.

Foster an Animal

Every year, hundreds of dogs and cats spend time growing and healing in our volunteer foster homes.

Join the Pet Hug Pack

We’re always looking for well-behaved pets to serve as therapy animals for people in need.

Start a New Career

Turn your passion into a career. Bring your expertise to our dedicated team.
